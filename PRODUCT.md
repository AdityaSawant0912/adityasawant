# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and fellow engineers landing on Aditya Sawant's personal portfolio, usually from a resume link, LinkedIn, or a referral. They scan quickly for proof of real, shipped work and a signal of taste. Context: short attention, often mobile, evaluating "is this person good and interesting?"

## Product Purpose
A personal portfolio for Aditya Sawant — full-stack software engineer (design systems → production APIs) with a creative-coding background. It exists to make him memorable and credible in the same glance: the content proves the engineering (68-component design system at millions of users, 4k-user EHS job board, OAuth pipelines), the design itself proves the taste and the generative/creative-coding side. Success = a visitor remembers the site and books a conversation.

## Brand Personality
Nocturnal, precise, alive. An engineer who is also an artist. Confident without shouting. The voice is direct and specific (real numbers, real systems), the surface is refined and a little uncanny — dark, luminous, motion that feels generated rather than decorated.

## Anti-references
- The AI-slop dark SaaS landing page (navy + gradient-text hero-metric cards).
- Editorial-magazine template (display-serif italic + drop caps + broadsheet grid) — not this brand.
- Generic resume-in-a-webpage: flat single column, no point of view.
- Glassmorphism-everywhere, identical icon+heading+text card grids.

## Design Principles
- **Show the craft, don't caption it.** The generative motion and blur are the argument for "creative coder," not a badge that says so.
- **Numbers carry the résumé.** Let concrete scale (millions of users, 4k, 500+/day, 68 components) do the persuading; keep copy terse.
- **Motion is generated, not sprinkled.** One coherent living system (flow field + drifting orbs + orchestrated reveals), not scattered micro-tricks.
- **Readable in the dark.** Luminosity and contrast first; effects never cost legibility.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast for all text. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` path: canvas + orbs freeze/fade, reveals become instant. Content lives in the DOM regardless of JS/motion state (SEO + no-JS safe). Keyboard-focusable interactive elements with visible focus.
